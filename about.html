About InfiniBot

InfiniBot was (initially) made as a meme and just for a single server. However, upon receiving feedback that InfiniBot could compete with other top-tier bots from server owners and fellow Discord members, InfiniBot was retrofitted to become what it is now. We were facing a dilemma. More often than not, some of the bots we had in their servers were often heavily paylocked. This wasn't ideal, so we decided to make a bot that we could control.

We wanted to develop a bot that does everything but sacrifices nothing.

Knowing the direction that data-collection was going in, we decided to stray away from the path. Here at InfiniBot, we only use necessary data, such as server IDs and User IDs. All user data is aggregated and anonymous, and only ONE person can access it. We NEVER give your information to third-party applications unless you choose to by using their services within InfiniBot.

At InfiniBot we also aim to bring as many of the premium features of other popular bots to a more pocket-friendly price: Free. You may feel that because it is free, the quality of the product is not as good. At InfiniBot, we have a commitment to make everything the best it can possibly be.
